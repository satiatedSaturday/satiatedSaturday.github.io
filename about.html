About Us

A Student-led Non-Profit Organization

"At Satiated Saturday, we firmly believe that a nutritious meal is more than just sustenance; it is a fundamental right that everyone deserves. We understand that food insecurity is a pressing issue, and we strive to alleviate the burden for those who are most vulnerable. By offering these meals, we aim to nourish both the body and spirit of those in need, fostering a sense of belonging and solidarity within our community.

Our organization is driven by a passionate team of dedicated student volunteers who are committed to making a difference. Ever 2 weeks, we come together to prepare and distribute nutritious, delicious meals to individuals who may otherwise go without. We prioritize the use of fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, ensuring that our meals are both wholesome and flavorful.
