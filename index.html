About us

A Student-led Non-Profit Organization

"Satiated Saturday" is a student-led non-profit organization dedicated to addressing food insecurity among the homeless and underprivileged individuals in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Our mission is to provide nourishing, homecooked meals to those in need, offering a sense of warmth, nutrition, and support to our local community.
